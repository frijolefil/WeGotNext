Our Values

The Women's
Game Changed
Everything

We're fans of the women's game — the athletes, styles, moves, sneaks, and memes. We approach this podcast as fans and social justice advocates.

We will uplift and center those who identify as women, and who are changing the game, on and off the court, the field, the pitch, the pool, and more.

We uphold workers over corporate greed, inclusivity and accessibility over exclusion, and equality over supremacy.

This is a safe space where we will learn and grow together as a community rooted in joy.

Why 'We Got Next'?

Women's basketball is on the rise and we want to tell that story from the fan experience. While there are a plethora of podcasts from athletes, journalists and other personalities, who is speaking exclusively for the fan experience? That's why We Got Next.

WNBA players are, and have been, at the forefront — an inspiration speaking out in the face of injustice. These women are leading with conviction and sophistication and we want to tell you all about it.

The Hosts

MH

Michelle Hatfield

Co-Host & Co-Founder

Michelle is a lifelong fan of women's sports who brings the energy, the takes, and the encyclopedic knowledge of every WNBA draft pick since 2003. She approaches the podcast as both a fan and a social justice advocate — believing that how we talk about women athletes matters as much as the game itself.

Off mic, Michelle is building community one conversation at a time.
